Zombie Survival!

The zombie horde is approaching... How will you fend them off?

Ways to deal with your family and closest friends becoming zombies right before your eyes...

<

Let's say you're walking along the street, trying to find provisions to help your survivor group in a zombie apocalypse stay alive. Out of nowhere, your best friend comes out of a dark alleyway, screaming "He bit me!". What would you do?

Well, in this situation, there are basically three things you can do. I have to warn you, though, none of them are pretty, and none of them will make you feel better in the end.

#1 is probably the most painful for both persons involved. Shoot them in the head as soon as they turn.

Now, you're probably saying "WHAT? I WOULD NEVER DO THAT!" Think about this: what if the person, who is just about to die and come back as a living nightmare, asks you to do it? Would you just deny them that wish? Probably not.

#2 is only bad for the person turning.

You could keep them with you, hoping that they won't turn into a zombie before some kind of cure is found/invented. All the while, the person who is turning is suffering pretty badly.

#3 is the most humane.

Some people would probably just give up if they were turning. They might ask you for a gun and to be taken to a nice, quiet place to pass on. At least this is a lot less painful and it's the person's choice.

See? Told ya none of these would be the prettiest or nicest way to end things, but if this does happen, they must be taken into consideration.
